# Cohort 2 Brief — Broader Users

**Size**: 75 testers
**Duration**: 2 weeks from invite
**Prerequisite**: Cohort 1 findings triaged. Critical issues fixed.

## Selection Criteria

- Developers, indie builders, technical founders
- Interested in agent-assisted work but not necessarily self-hosters
- Mix of: solo developers, small team leads, startup CTOs

## Testing Brief

1. **Get Plexo running**: Either self-host or use the hosted preview instance.
2. **Run at least 3 tasks** of your choice. No category requirement.
3. **Answer four questions** after your testing:
   - Did the agent do what you asked?
   - Did you understand what it produced?
   - Would you use this weekly?
   - What's the one thing that would make you recommend it to someone else?

## Feedback Deliverable

Complete the feedback form within the 2-week window. Include at least one specific task example.

## Gate Criteria

When the majority of Cohort 2 answers **Yes** to "Did the agent do what you asked?" and "Did you understand what it produced?", the public announcement gate is unlocked.
